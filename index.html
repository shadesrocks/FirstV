Frequently Asked Questions

How many leads will I get?

You may be wondering how many leads you can expect to receive through our referral network. The volume of leads can vary depending on the specific market you are targeting and your conversion rate. On average, we provide 3-5 referrals per agent per month.

How does we get leads?

We use over 150 sources to generate traffic for real estate professionals, which are mainly categorized into three groups:
•    Search engine optimization (SEO) efforts on platforms such as Google and Yahoo.
•    Collaboration with website influencers, bloggers, and website networks.
•    Pay-per-click (PPC) marketing and social media advertising on platforms like Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn.
In addition, we also reach out to expired listings and for-sale-by-owner properties to provide a mix of both buyer and seller leads.

What is the quality of the lead?  

At Falcon Technology , we believe in quality over quantity when it comes to our referral leads. We get paid per closing, not per lead, so we prioritize sending you leads that are more likely to close. We ensure that each lead we send you has expressed a genuine interest in buying or selling property by collecting key details about their timeline, budget, asking price, and more.

How the leads are transferred ?

At Falcon Technology, we transfer all referrals to real estate agents in real-time through one of our support managers. Alternatively, if preferred, appointment details can be shared via email or text message.
We prioritize making the transfer process as seamless and efficient as possible for our real estate partners.

How can I choose my preferred zip codes?

The best part about our network is that you can choose your desired locations. You can select your ideal zip codes with ease! Our network offers you the freedom to pick the areas you want to work on, based on your preferred plan. Specify your desired radius of 15-50 miles, and we'll geo-target your selected territory.
